Registration

When you register a complaint in the designated menu, you will be provided with a detailed outline of the process.

Initially, your complaint will be assessed to determine the relevant department. For instance, issues related to crime will be forwarded to the police, while garbage-related concerns will go to the Municipal Corporation.

Once assigned, you will receive the contact details of the officer or representative responsible for handling your case, enabling you to follow up on your complaint directly.

Emergency Helpline Numbers

For urgent matters, there is a dedicated section listing all emergency helpline numbers. This includes contacts for police, medical emergencies, fire services, and more, ensuring you can act swiftly in critical situations.
